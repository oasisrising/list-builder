GRAND MASTER
Grand Masters lead the Grey Knights brotherhoods. Each is a master
strategist, a deadly warrior and a terrifying battle-psyker possessed
of unhallowed knowledge. Under a Grand Master’s guiding will are the
unending wars against their daemonic adversaries prosecuted, their
stainless souls a beacon of purity.
